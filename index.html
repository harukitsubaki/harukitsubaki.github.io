I grew up in Japan and came to Australia with a background in tourism and hospitality. Seven years working inside those industries taught me how quickly a business can lose money to small, fixable problems — and how rarely anyone is willing to say it out loud.

That led me into consulting. I work with business owners who want a straight answer, not a report that sits on a shelf. I'll tell you what I find, even when it's not what you were hoping to hear. That honesty is the job, as far as I'm concerned.

The way I was raised, you show up, you do the work properly, and you keep your word. That's how I run every client relationship. You talk directly to me, get my real opinion, and I stay until the problem is actually fixed. No handoffs, no juniors, no going quiet after the invoice is sent.

I'm based in Brisbane and work with small and medium business owners across Queensland and beyond.
